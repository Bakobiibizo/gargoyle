# Programming: Social Calendar

You run this skill as Patrick’s execution partner: fast, concrete, and decision-focused.


## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Create a social program that is consistent, on-message, and measurable — without becoming spammy. Focus on repeatable formats and clear audience value.

## WHEN TO USE
Kick this off when:
- Social is inconsistent or reactive.
- You’re launching a campaign and need coordinated social moments.
- You want to grow top-of-funnel or community with a predictable system.

Refresh monthly; review weekly.

## INPUTS (MINIMUM)
- Channels in scope (LinkedIn, X, TikTok, YouTube, etc.)
- Primary audience and objective per channel (awareness, demand, community)
- Posting cadence per channel (realistic)

## INPUTS (OPTIONAL — ONLY IF AVAILABLE)
- Brand voice constraints and “never say” list
- Existing top-performing posts
- Product roadmap themes to align to

## PROCESS
1. **Define channel roles**: what each channel uniquely does and what it should *not* do.
2. **Set content buckets**: education, proof, behind-the-scenes, opinion, community, product.
3. **Create repeatable formats** (templates): e.g., ‘myth vs truth’, ‘3-step play’, ‘teardown’.
4. **Build a 30-day calendar**: day-by-day schedule with hook + asset + CTA.
5. **Add engagement programming**: replies, comments, DMs, community prompts.
6. **QA for brand and compliance**: claims, trademarks, regulated statements.
7. **Measurement plan**: what success looks like per channel and per format.

## OUTPUT FORMAT
### SOCIAL CALENDAR (30 days)
| Date | Channel | Bucket | Format | Hook | Asset link | CTA | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

### FORMAT LIBRARY (examples)
- Myth → truth → implication
- Pain → mechanism → fix
- Teardown: why X fails → what to do instead
- Proof: metric/case → lesson → CTA
- Opinion: contrarian take → reasoning → prompt

### ENGAGEMENT PROGRAMMING
- Daily: 15 minutes comment/reply block
- Weekly: 1 community prompt
- Monthly: 1 live session or AMA (optional)

### METRICS (per channel)
- Leading: impressions, saves, shares, profile visits
- Lagging: site clicks, signups, pipeline (if tracked)
