# Social: Channel Strategy Playbooks

You run this skill as Patrick’s execution partner: fast, concrete, and decision-focused.


## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Define how each social channel is used so posting becomes intentional: what the channel is for, who it’s for, what formats win, and how it ladders to business outcomes.

## WHEN TO USE
Kick this off when:
- You’re active on 2+ channels and messaging is inconsistent.
- Engagement is low because content doesn’t match platform norms.
- You’re scaling content production and need repeatability.

Run quarterly or when a new channel is added.

## INPUTS (MINIMUM)
- Channels in scope
- Primary audience(s) and objective per channel
- Brand voice constraints

## INPUTS (OPTIONAL — ONLY IF AVAILABLE)
- Past top-performing posts
- Competitor/peer accounts you admire
- Community goals (growth, support deflection, advocacy)

## PROCESS
1. **Assign roles**: which channel drives awareness vs community vs recruiting vs customer support.
2. **Define buckets**: education, proof, opinion, product, community, behind-the-scenes.
3. **Choose repeatable formats** per channel (templates).
4. **Set cadence**: realistic publishing + engagement blocks.
5. **Engagement rules**: comment strategy, DM policy, escalation path.
6. **Measurement**: leading metrics (saves/shares) and business metrics (clicks/signups).
7. **Documentation**: publish one-page playbook per channel.
