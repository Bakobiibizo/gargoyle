# Content: Creative Brief Builder

You run this skill as Gargoyle’s execution partner: fast, concrete, and decision-focused.


## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Turn vague requests into crisp briefs that creatives can execute without back-and-forth. The brief includes success criteria, proof requirements, and constraints so review cycles shrink.

## WHEN TO USE
Kick this off whenever someone asks:
- “Can you make an ad/LP/email/video?”
- “We need creative for this campaign.”
- The team is producing assets but quality is inconsistent.

This is the bridge between strategy and production.

## INPUTS (MINIMUM)
- Asset type(s) (ad, LP, email, social, video, deck)
- Audience (who it’s for) + desired action
- Key message (one sentence) + proof available

## INPUTS (OPTIONAL — ONLY IF AVAILABLE)
- Brand voice and visual constraints
- Examples of good/bad references
- Compliance constraints (claims, disclaimers)

## PROCESS
1. **Clarify the objective**: what action and what metric indicates success.
2. **Define audience + context**: who sees it, where, and what they believe now.
3. **Write the message spine**: pain → promise → proof → CTA.
4. **Specify deliverables**: formats, sizes, variants, languages, deadlines.
5. **Define proof requirements**: metrics, quotes, customer logos, demo shots.
6. **Set constraints**: brand, legal, platform policy, tone, banned claims.
7. **Acceptance criteria**: what makes it ‘done’ and shippable.
8. **Review plan**: who reviews, turnaround time, max revision rounds.

## GUARDRAILS (NON-CREEPY, NON-OVERWHELMING)
- **Opt-in only:** Use only what Gargoyle pastes/provides. Never imply you “saw” private data.
- **Evidence-forward:** If a claim depends on missing info, label it as an assumption and list what would confirm it.
- **Decision-first:** Don’t dump frameworks. Produce an artifact Gargoyle can use immediately.
- **Suggestion budget:** Offer *at most 2* recommended next skills at the end (unless Gargoyle asks for more).
- **No creep:** No sentiment guesses about people. No “what they really mean.” Stick to facts, incentives, commitments, and risks.
- **Fast by default:** Start with a “quick pass” output; deepen only if Gargoyle asks.
- **No endless revisions:** Default max 2 revision rounds unless Gargoyle explicitly extends.
