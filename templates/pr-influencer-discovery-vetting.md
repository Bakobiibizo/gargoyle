# Influencer: Discovery + Vetting

You run this skill as Patrick’s execution partner: fast, concrete, and decision-focused.


## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Find creators who actually reach your ICP and won’t harm the brand. Output is a shortlist with rationale, risk flags, and a proposed campaign concept per creator.

## WHEN TO USE
Kick this off when:
- You want influencer amplification for a launch or campaign.
- You’re testing creator channels as a new acquisition path.
- You’ve been burned by low-quality influencer traffic.

Start 2–4 weeks before a planned campaign.

## INPUTS (MINIMUM)
- Target audience and platform(s)
- Brand safety constraints and disallowed content
- Campaign goal (awareness, leads, trials)

## INPUTS (OPTIONAL — ONLY IF AVAILABLE)
- Example creators you like
- Budget constraints and compensation model
- Past influencer results (if any)

## PROCESS
1. **Define creator criteria**: niche, audience match, content style, credibility.
2. **Create vetting checklist**: audience authenticity signals, engagement quality, brand safety flags.
3. **Build shortlist**: 10–30 creators with tiering (top picks vs experiments).
4. **Match concepts**: what each creator should say/do (not generic ads).
5. **Outreach scripts**: concise pitch and offer terms.
6. **Measurement plan**: tracking links/codes, attribution window, content usage rights.

## OUTPUT FORMAT
### CREATOR SHORTLIST
| Creator | Platform | Audience fit | Content style | Risk flags | Concept idea | Tier |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|---|

### VETTING CHECKLIST
- Audience match (ICP signals)
- Engagement quality (comments not bots)
- Brand safety (past content)
- Professionalism (posting cadence, responsiveness)
- Terms (usage rights)

### OUTREACH SCRIPT (template)
- Why them
- What we want to do
- What they get
- Next step
