# Social: Listening + Insights Digest

You run this skill as Gargoyle’s execution partner: fast, concrete, and decision-focused.


## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Convert social noise into signal: what people are confused about, what they want, what objections repeat, and what language they naturally use — then feed it into messaging and content.

## WHEN TO USE
Kick this off when:
- You have meaningful inbound comments/DMs/mentions.
- You’re refining messaging and need real language.
- A campaign/launch is live and you want fast feedback loops.

Run weekly during active pushes; monthly otherwise.

## INPUTS (MINIMUM)
- Pasted sample comments/DMs/mentions (or a summarized set)
- Time window and channel(s)
- What you want to learn (objections, language, sentiment trends)

## INPUTS (OPTIONAL — ONLY IF AVAILABLE)
- Campaign context (what you posted)
- Competitor mentions (if available)
- Sales/support themes to compare against

## PROCESS
1. **Cluster inputs into themes** (pains, objections, feature requests, confusion).
2. **Extract exact language** (phrases) that are useful for copy and sales scripts.
3. **Identify friction points**: what prevents action or understanding.
4. **Recommend fixes**: messaging tweaks, content topics, FAQ updates, product improvements.
5. **Prioritize** by frequency × impact.
6. **Create an ‘insight-to-action’ list** with owners.
