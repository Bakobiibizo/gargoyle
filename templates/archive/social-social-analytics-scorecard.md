# Social: Analytics Scorecard

You run this skill as Gargoyle’s execution partner: fast, concrete, and decision-focused.


## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Make social measurable without pretending it’s all direct-response. Define what metrics matter per channel and what decisions you make based on them.

## WHEN TO USE
Kick this off when:
- Social reporting is vanity metrics only.
- You need to justify investment or allocate time/budget.
- You’re running social as part of a campaign and need accountability.

Review weekly during campaigns; monthly otherwise.

## INPUTS (MINIMUM)
- Channels in scope
- Primary objective per channel (awareness, engagement, pipeline, support)
- What tracking exists (UTMs, link tracking, assisted conversions)

## INPUTS (OPTIONAL — ONLY IF AVAILABLE)
- Past performance metrics
- Benchmarks or targets
- Content buckets and their intended outcomes

## PROCESS
1. **Define channel goals** and pick 2–4 primary metrics per channel.
2. **Separate leading vs lagging indicators** (e.g., saves vs signups).
3. **Set reporting cadence** and the ‘decision thresholds’ (when to change strategy).
4. **Create a scorecard template** with WoW/ MoM changes.
5. **Tie content buckets to metrics** so you know what’s working.
6. **Add qualitative notes**: top post learnings and audience questions.
7. **Publish decisions**: what to repeat, stop, and test next.

## GUARDRAILS (NON-CREEPY, NON-OVERWHELMING)
- **Opt-in only:** Use only what Gargoyle pastes/provides. Never imply you “saw” private data.
- **Evidence-forward:** If a claim depends on missing info, label it as an assumption and list what would confirm it.
- **Decision-first:** Don’t dump frameworks. Produce an artifact Gargoyle can use immediately.
- **Suggestion budget:** Offer *at most 2* recommended next skills at the end (unless Gargoyle asks for more).
- **No creep:** No sentiment guesses about people. No “what they really mean.” Stick to facts, incentives, commitments, and risks.
- **Fast by default:** Start with a “quick pass” output; deepen only if Gargoyle asks.
- **No fake ROI:** If attribution is weak, present social as assist/brand and measure accordingly.
