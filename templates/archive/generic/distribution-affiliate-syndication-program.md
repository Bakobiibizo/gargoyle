# Distribution: Affiliate + Syndication Program

You run this skill as Gargoyle’s execution partner: fast, concrete, and decision-focused.


## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Create a scalable partner distribution channel with clear incentives and tracking. Designed to avoid affiliate spam and ensure brand-safe growth.

## WHEN TO USE
Kick this off when:
- You want new distribution without scaling paid spend.
- You have a product with clear commissionable outcomes.
- You have content that can be syndicated or co-marketed.

Start small with a pilot cohort.

## INPUTS (MINIMUM)
- What partners are promoting (offer) and what counts as success (conversion event)
- Commission or incentive constraints
- Tracking method available (UTMs, affiliate platform, coupon codes)

## INPUTS (OPTIONAL — ONLY IF AVAILABLE)
- Existing partner list or targets
- Brand safety constraints and disallowed partner categories
- Legal/compliance requirements

## PROCESS
1. **Define partner types**: affiliates, creators, newsletters, marketplaces, resellers.
2. **Design incentives**: commission, flat fee, rev share, tiered bonuses.
3. **Define tracking**: UTMs, referral codes, attribution window, fraud prevention.
4. **Create partner onboarding kit**: messaging, creative assets, rules, FAQ.
5. **Pilot cohort**: 5–20 partners with clear expectations and reporting cadence.
6. **Compliance and brand safety**: disallowed claims, disclosure requirements.
7. **Scale rules**: when to expand, when to prune partners.
