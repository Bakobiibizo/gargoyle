# Content: Video Production Plan

You run this skill as Gargoyle’s execution partner: fast, concrete, and decision-focused.


## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Plan and produce video assets efficiently: define the concept, script, production checklist, and distribution cuts so video becomes repeatable rather than a one-off project.

## WHEN TO USE
Kick this off when:
- You’re producing product demos, ads, explainers, founder videos, or customer stories.
- You need video variants for paid/social.
- You want a repeatable video workflow.

Start 1–3 weeks ahead depending on complexity.

## INPUTS (MINIMUM)
- Video purpose (awareness, demo, conversion) and target audience
- Core message and CTA
- Distribution channel(s) (paid social, website, YouTube)

## INPUTS (OPTIONAL — ONLY IF AVAILABLE)
- Available talent (founder, customer, team)
- Existing footage or UI captures
- Brand constraints and examples

## PROCESS
1. **Pick the format**: talking head, screen-record demo, UGC-style, animated.
2. **Write the script** with hook-first structure and proof integrated.
3. **Create a shot list** and b-roll requirements.
4. **Define roles**: director/producer/editor/reviewer.
5. **Plan the edit**: pacing, captions, on-screen text, music constraints.
6. **Create cut-down plan**: 60s, 30s, 15s, 6s, plus thumbnails.
7. **QA**: captions, claims, brand, audio levels, aspect ratios.
8. **Distribution plan**: publish schedule + A/B test hooks/thumbnails.
