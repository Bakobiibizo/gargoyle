# Partnerships: Sourcing + Co-Marketing

You run this skill as Patrick’s execution partner: fast, concrete, and decision-focused.


## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Create partnerships that drive distribution: identify the right partners, pitch a value exchange, and execute co-marketing with clear tracking and shared success metrics.

## WHEN TO USE
Kick this off when:
- You want new distribution without pure paid spend.
- Integrations or ecosystem partners can accelerate adoption.
- You’re planning a joint webinar, bundle, or announcement.

Start with a pilot of 3–5 partners.

## INPUTS (MINIMUM)
- Partner types (integration partners, agencies, platforms, communities)
- What you can offer partners (audience, content, rev share, integration)
- Desired outcome (leads, adoption, credibility)

## INPUTS (OPTIONAL — ONLY IF AVAILABLE)
- Existing partner list
- Integration roadmap
- Legal constraints (data sharing, co-selling)

## PROCESS
1. **Define ideal partner profile**: audience overlap, distribution power, reputation.
2. **Create value exchange**: what they get and what you get (explicit).
3. **Build outreach messaging** and a short partner pitch deck (5 slides).
4. **Design co-marketing package**: webinar, newsletter swap, joint case study, bundle.
5. **Tracking plan**: UTMs/referral codes; attribution window; lead ownership rules.
6. **Pilot execution**: run 1–2 co-marketing motions per partner.
7. **Review and scale**: prune low performers, expand winners.
